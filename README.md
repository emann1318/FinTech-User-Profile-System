# Vestor - Pakistan Investment Planning

Vestor is a specialized financial planning and investment tracking platform designed specifically for the Pakistani market. It provides users with an intuitive interface to explore, simulate, and manage financial assets ranging from National Savings and Corporate Bonds to High-Growth Tech Indices and Cryptocurrency.

## Key Features

### 1. Smart Recommendation Engine
Receive personalized investment suggestions based on your unique financial profile. The engine evaluates:
*   **Risk Tolerance**: Conservative, Moderate, or Aggressive.
*   **Investment Horizon**: Short, Medium, or Long term.
*   **Monthly Capacity**: Budget-aware suggestions tailored to PKR limits.
*   **Liquidity Preferences**: Ensuring your capital is available when you need it.

### 2. Risk Profile Quiz
An interactive 1-minute assessment tool that helps you understand your "Investor Persona." Results are instantly synchronized with your profile to tailor the entire marketplace experience.

### 3. Quick Financial Tools
Built-in utilities for the local context:
*   **Zakat Calculator**: Instant calculation of the 2.5% wealth obligation.
*   **FBR Tax Estimator**: Quick projections based on standard income slabs.

### 4. Interactive Portfolio
Simulate allocations and track "Weighted Yield" across your various assets. The portfolio provides a visual breakdown of your risk exposure and projected maturity values.

### 5. Localized Asset Marketplace
A curated fed of financial instruments transformed from live market data, featuring:
*   **Savings**: National Savings Certificates, Meezan & HBL Savings plans.
*   **Investments**: PSX Top 100 Index, Alfalah Funds, Corporate Bonds.
*   **Insurance**: EFU Life, Jubilee, and Takaful plans.
*   **Tech Assets**: Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Solana portfolios.

##  Design Theme: "Artistic Flair"
The application uses a distinctive, professional design aesthetic centered around **Vestor Green (#2e7d32)**. 
*   **Typography**: Clean, high-performance Segoe UI/Sans-Serif.
*   **Structure**: Professional sidebar-driven layout for high information density.
*   **Accents**: Left-accented headers and "boxy" card styles (4px radius) for a unique, human-crafted feel.

##  Technical Stack
*   **Frontend**: React (JavaScript)
*   **Styling**: CSS
*   **Icons**:  React
*   **Data Source**: FakeStoreAPI (Transformed via a custom Financial Adapter)
*   **Deployment**: Optimized for standard Node.js environments.
